JP Manabat

Freelance graphic design work to promote professional dancer in Toronto

2024

Photoshop

Created by Toronto-based dancer JP Manabat, ANBU is a private dance program that offers education on street dance. Integrating aesthetics that mimick one of the pilars of Hip Hop: graffiti, I created social media graphics for the program reflecting the integrity of JP's dance styles: Hip Hop and Breaking.

ANBU Project is currently in the works, anticipating a public launch in 2025.
