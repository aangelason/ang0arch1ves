Poster Design for Angelique Dancel's live EP performance: With Love (A.D.) Using doodles and unique typefaces, the work focuses on capturing the singers's artistry inspired by her love for hand-written journals. The design reflects her intimate, heartfelt storytelling, inviting audiences into her world of music and personal expression.
