Graphic Design, Digital Art, Movement Art

Angela is a multidisciplinary artist based in Toronto, Ontario specializing in graphic design, illustration, and creative coding. Her main interests are digital media, web design/development, and art direction.

Outside of design, she dedicates a lot of her time in dance, drawing inspiration from the art of movement and the Toronto dance community. She integrates her diverse creative influence into her designs, bringing a dynamic quality to her work.

Angela is currently studying New Media at Toronto Metropolitan University, where she explores innovative approaches to digital storytelling, design, and technology.
