Anastomosis

Logo and poster design for student art exhibition

2024

Photoshop, Procreate, Illustrator

Designers: Angela Son, Madeline Dam Hoang, Lucah Huising Torrese

Logo and Poster Design for Anastomosis, an art exhibition led by the New Media Exhibition Team. The focus of this design relied on the definition of anastomosis, in which it entails a connection between two things that are normally diverging away from each other - like blood vessels.
