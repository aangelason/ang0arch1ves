Crazy Sock Lady is an experimental digital media project that integrates innovative methods into mundane everyday objects such as socks. Showcasing different digital experiences with each drop, Crazy Sock Lady aims to bring a memorable value to customers. In this particular sock game experience, I developed hand-drawn illustrations and graphic design game assets that evoke a dark, twisted imagery of the Old West. Blending in a "Black Mirror"-esque lens, the visual explores the enduring unfairness of life—a recurring theme in the American Frontier that continues to resonate in modern society.
